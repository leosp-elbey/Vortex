# VORTEXTRIPS ORGANIC LEAD ENGINE

Goal:
Generate 20 qualified organic leads per day.

Lead definition:
A qualified lead is a real person who has shown interest in travel savings, a travel membership, or the affiliate program and has enough contact information for follow-up.

Core lead paths:
1. Social content to /free
2. DM conversation to /free
3. Free page to email nurture
4. Email nurture to /book
5. Booking to presentation
6. Presentation to /join
7. Join to onboarding
8. Onboarding to referral

Daily lead activities:
- Publish or schedule content
- Respond to comments
- Send approved DMs
- Follow up with old leads
- Invite interested people to /free
- Move qualified prospects to /book
- Recover no-shows
- Follow up on abandoned joins
- Ask activated members for referrals

Lead sources:
- Facebook
- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts
- LinkedIn
- Google Business Profile
- Email list
- SMS list where compliant
- Referral partners
- Existing contacts
- Travel groups
- Local community groups
